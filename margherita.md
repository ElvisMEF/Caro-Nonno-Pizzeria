# Margherita Pizza

**Ingredients:**
- Pizza dough
- Tomato sauce
- Fresh mozzarella
- Fresh basil
- Olive oil
- Salt

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Spread tomato sauce over dough.
3. Add mozzarella, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.
4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
